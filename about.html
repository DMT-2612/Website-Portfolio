About

I am interested in how teams, processes, and systems come together to solve real business problems.

I work across business analysis, workflow design, and project coordination to help teams turn ambiguity into structured execution. My focus is on understanding business needs, analysing processes, and translating requirements into practical solutions teams can actually use.

Across different projects, I have contributed through process mapping, stakeholder collaboration, workflow redesign, wireframing, and delivery coordination. I enjoy creating structure within messy environments and aligning people around clear, actionable outcomes.
